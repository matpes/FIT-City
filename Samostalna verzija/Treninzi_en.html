TRAININGS

 

YOGA

Yoga is an ancient method of self-development, originally from India, which involves the use of techniques: physical postures, energy management exercises through breathing, relaxation-meditative exercises, concentration, meditation and self-realization.

PILATES

Pilates is a refined form of exercise that simultaneously develops specific muscle strength as well as flexibility of muscles and joints, thereby achieving optimal body balance. Pilates lengthens and tones the body, influences elegant posture, relieves stress, contributes to greater self-control and greater self-confidence.

CORE

Core exercises are designed to strengthen the muscles, tendons and ligaments of the abdomen. These exercises, along with back exercises, are key to maintaining your body torso. A well-maintained torso (abdominal and back muscles surrounding the spine) is one of the best ways to maintain good posture when aging and one of the best defenses against frequent back injuries that cause pain.

CARDI O

Cardio training is the most effective way to remove fat, achieve and maintain fitness. Cardio exercises are necessary for any effective training program as they promote fat burning but are also good for the overall health of the body.
