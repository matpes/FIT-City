FIT CITY


FIT CITY is a city fitness specialist with more than 200 fitness classes per month and 12 different types of programs. From Les Mills to yoga, Pilates, cardio ...
The FIT CITY Fitness Club was created to give its members the feel and benefits of sports.
Naša filozofija je bazirana na humanističkim i sportskim vrednostima a naša ekipa profesionalaca je uvek spremna da vas dočeka, sasluša i pruži podršku. To je ekipa uz koju ćete se i zabaviti i dobiti željene rezultate.
Our philosophy is based on humanistic and sporting values and our team of professionals is always ready to welcome you, listen to you and support you. This is a team you will have fun with and get the results you want.
We are specialists in group fitness programs, but we also hold unforgettable parties and fitness events!
